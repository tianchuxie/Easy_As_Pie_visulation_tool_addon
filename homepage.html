EasyAsPie is meant to be an application for beginner- to medium-level website developers who would like to provide their customers with a better visualization experience. It is designed to increase navigational efficiency by reducing the number of mouse actions required from client-side users. It will also be efficient for the developers who will, with minimal steps, input desired data into this tool so that an organized and efficient data visualization may be created for their possession. The dataset that will be entered into the pie chart, and will be in the form of a csv file. The programming languages used will be SQL and Javascript, as well as, HTML and CSS.
